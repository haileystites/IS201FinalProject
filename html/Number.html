How to Play

  • Guess a number between 1 and 100 in the input field.
  • You have a maximum of 10 attempts to guess the correct number.
  • After each guess, you'll get feedback if your guess was too high or too low.
  • Use the "Get Hint" button for a helpful clue (uses one attempt).
  • Try to guess the number in as few attempts as possible to get a high score!
